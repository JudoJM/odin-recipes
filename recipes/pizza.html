PIZZA

the favorite dish of many, they have a wide variety of ingredients

In this recipe we using few ingredients

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough (you can buy pre-made dough or make it yourself)
  • Tomato sauce
  • Grated mozzarella cheese
  • Additional toppings of your choice (e.g., pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, onions, etc.)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 220°C (425°F).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a baking sheet.
  3. Spread tomato sauce evenly over the dough, leaving about a 1 cm border.
  4. Generously sprinkle grated mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
  5. Add your favorite toppings over the cheese.
  6. Bake the pizza in the preheated oven for about 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly.
  7. Remove from the oven, let it cool for a few minutes, slice into portions , and enjoy your delicious homemade pizza!
