LASAGNA

Description

In this recipe we go to create a lasagna, this will be funny and delicious

This job will be easy if you follow the instructions correctly.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef or Italian sausage
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Italian seasoning
  • Optional: chopped onion and garlic

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a skillet, cook the ground beef or Italian sausage over medium heat until browned. Drain excess fat if needed.
  4. Add tomato sauce to the cooked meat, along with salt, pepper, Italian seasoning, and any optional chopped onion and garlic. Simmer for a few minutes.
  5. In a separate bowl, mix together ricotta cheese, shredded mozzarella cheese, and grated Parmesan cheese.
  6. In a baking dish, layer the ingredients in the following order: a thin layer of meat sauce, lasagna noodles, ricotta cheese mixture, and repeat until all ingredients are used, ending with a layer of meat sauce on top.
  7. Cover the baking dish with foil and bake in the preheated oven for about 30 minutes.
  8. Remove the foil and continue baking for an additional 10-15 minutes, or until the lasagna is bubbly and the cheese is golden brown on top.
  9. Let it cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.
